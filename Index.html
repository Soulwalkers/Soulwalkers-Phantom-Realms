Game Rules

Welcome to Soulwalkers: Phantom Realms! Here, players harness the power of spirits to engage in strategic card battles. The objective is to defeat your opponents by reducing their spirit energy to zero.

Basic Gameplay:

  • Each player starts with a deck of 30 cards.
  • Players draw 5 cards at the beginning of the game.
  • On your turn, you can summon spirits, cast spells, or attack.
  • The game ends when one player's spirit energy reaches zero.

Special Abilities:

Many spirits have unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle. Use them wisely!

Lore

In a realm where the living and the spirits coexist, the balance between worlds is delicate. As a Soulwalker, you possess the unique ability to summon and command spirits from the Phantom Realms.

Long ago, a great cataclysm shattered the barriers between the living and the dead, allowing powerful spirits to roam freely. Now, it's up to you to harness their power and restore balance, or plunge the realms into chaos.
